Space Marines Stratagems

Rapid Fire - 1 CP

Use in the Shooting phase when a unit shoots. Each bolt weapon that model is equipped with makes 1 additional attack.

Phase: Shooting

Death to the Traitors! - 1 CP

Use in the Fight phase when a Space Marines unit fights. Each time a model in that unit makes a melee attack, add 1 to the hit roll.

Phase: Fight

Armour of Contempt - 1 CP

Use when a Space Marines unit is targeted by an attack. Improve the Armour Save characteristic of models in that unit by 1 for that attack.

Phase: Any

Tactical Doctrine - 1 CP

Use at the start of any turn. Select the Tactical Doctrine. Until the end of that turn, improve the Armour Penetration characteristic of Rapid Fire and Assault weapons by 1.

Phase: Command

Honour the Chapter - 2 CP

Use in the Fight phase when a Space Marines unit finishes making its attacks. That unit can immediately fight again.

Phase: Fight

Steady Advance - 1 CP

Use in the Movement phase when a Space Marines Infantry unit Advances. That unit can still shoot this turn, but only with Assault weapons.

Phase: Movement

Heroic Intervention - 2 CP

Use in the Charge phase when an enemy unit ends a charge move within 1" of one of your Character units. That Character can perform a Heroic Intervention.

Phase: Charge

Combat Revival - 1 CP

Use at the start of the Morale phase. Select one of your units that is not at its starting strength. Return 1 slain model to that unit.

Phase: Morale

Orbital Bombardment - 3 CP

Use in the Shooting phase if a Space Marines Character has not moved this turn. Select one enemy unit within 48" and roll a D6 for each model in that unit. Each 6 inflicts 1 mortal wound.

Phase: Shooting

Lightning Assault - 2 CP

Use in the Charge phase when a Space Marines unit declares a charge. That unit can charge even if it Advanced this turn.

Phase: Charge
